Welcome to Valmi Products

My name is Dhananjay Badave, and I proudly hold the position of CEO. By qualification, I am an Engineer with a wealth of experience in Development, Purchase, and PPC. My journey began at MICO, Nasik, where I dedicated a year honing my skills. Following that, I spent an enriching 14 years at BAJAJ AUTO LIMITED in Waluj, where I further refined my expertise. In 2005, I made a pivotal decision to transition into entrepreneurship, marking the first in my family to embark on this path. This leap into business ownership has been a fulfilling and transformative experience, allowing me to blend my technical acumen with strategic vision to drive success.

Succession & The Future

Mr. Nishant Badave completed his graduation in Production Engineering from the prestigious College of Engineering Pune (COEP) in 2020-21. Demonstrating his commitment to our family legacy, he joined our business in August 2023. As the torchbearer of our succession plan, Nishant brings a fresh perspective and unwavering dedication to driving our business forward.
